Manali Adventure Trip 2026

Hyderabad → Delhi → Manali → Rohtang → Solang → Jispa → Sissu → Kasol → Manikaran → Back Home.

A breathtaking 10-day backpacking adventure filled with snowy mountains, riverside cafés, Tibetan food, paragliding, rafting, scenic road trips, hidden valleys, hot springs, unforgettable sunsets and cozy Himalayan nights.

Explore Journey ↓

✨ Your Complete Travel Timeline ✨

Every destination, café, activity, ride and detail beautifully planned.

🚆 Day 1 — Hyderabad → Delhi
☀️ Morning: Pack essentials, water bottles and light snacks for the train journey.
🌤️ Afternoon: Board train at 4:20 PM from Hyderabad to Delhi.
🌙 Evening: Overnight train journey begins.
🚌 Day 2 — Delhi → Manali
☀️ Morning: Arrive in Delhi → Take Metro to Kashmiri Gate ISBT.
🌙 Evening: Board overnight Volvo/Bus to Manali.
🏡 Day 3 — Settle Into Manali
☀️ Morning: Arrive in Manali → Check into budget hotel → Rest and acclimatize.
🌤️ Afternoon: Walk around Old Manali → Riverside chill and photography.
🌙 Evening: Café hopping + dinner.
🍜 Tenzin Dhaba — Budget Tibetan comfort food famous for momos and thukpa.

☕ Dylan's Toasted & Roasted Coffee House — Cozy café with good coffee, desserts and light bites.
🛕 Day 4 — Temples, Snow & Hot Springs
☀️ Morning: Visit Rohtang Pass (RTC Himachal Pradesh).
🌤️ Afternoon: Explore Hadimba Devi Temple → Forest photo-walk nearby.
🌙 Evening: Relax in Vashisht Hot Water Springs → Dinner at Cafe 1947.
🌊 Cafe 1947 — Beautiful riverside ambience.
Budget tip: Order 1 main + 1 drink and share dessert.
🪂 Day 5 — Adventure Day
☀️ Morning: Explore scenic Koksar.
🌤️ Afternoon: Adventure activities:
• Paragliding
• Zipline
• ATV rides
🌙 Evening: Spend sunset time at Solang Valley.
☕ Solang Valley is more about snack stalls and dhabas than sit-down cafés. Expect tea, coffee and quick snacks like Maggi.
🚘 Day 6 — Atal Tunnel & Sissu Trip
☀️ Morning: Visit Jispa.
🌤️ Afternoon: Explore Sissu and surrounding viewpoints.
🌙 Evening: Return to Manali → Dinner at Rocky's Cafe.
🍽️ Rocky's Cafe — Casual café-style place perfect for a relaxed dinner after a long drive.
🚣 Day 7 — River Rafting & Cafés
☀️ Morning: Beas River rafting on the Babeli–Pirdi stretch.
🌤️ Afternoon: Recovery time in Old Manali → Café hopping and bakery stop.
🌙 Evening: Dinner at Monastery Cafe.
🍲 Monastery Cafe — Calm atmosphere and budget-friendly warm meals.
🏕️ Day 8 — Kasol & Manikaran
☀️ Morning: Govt bus from Manali to Kasol → Rent bike.
🌤️ Afternoon: Visit Manikaran and Manikaran Sahib.
🌙 Evening: Dinner at Cafe By The Hidden Tribe → Overnight stay at Drifter's Inn & Cafe.
☕ Cafe By The Hidden Tribe — Relaxed Kasol vibe and perfect dinner stop.

🏡 Drifter's Inn & Cafe — Convenient backpacker stay + café combo.
🚌 Day 9 — Kasol → Delhi
☀️ Morning: Bus from Kasol to Delhi.
🌤️ Afternoon: Buffer for traffic and reaching railway station.
🌙 Evening: Train from Delhi to Hyderabad.
🏁 Day 10 — Arrive Hyderabad
🌤️ Arrival Day: Reach Hyderabad safely.
✨ Trip Ends: Mountains, rivers, cafés, adventures and unforgettable memories completed successfully.

💙 The Ultimate Himalayan Backpacking Experience

From overnight train rides and snowy Himalayan roads to riverside cafés, hot springs, rafting adventures and peaceful mountain sunsets — this journey blends adventure, relaxation, budget travel and unforgettable memories. Every day has its own vibe, every café has its own story and every destination adds another chapter to your Himalayan adventure.
